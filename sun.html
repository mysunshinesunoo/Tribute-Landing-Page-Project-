Pre-Debut

  • Sunoo trained under Belift Lab for 10 months prior to participating in I-Land.
  • During Be:Lift’s audition for I-Land, he beat 199 other contestants to be selected for the reality show.
  • With his love for singing and dancing, he actively participates in his middle school’s annual choir competition.
  • Sunoo attended Chilbo Middle School, where he was a choir student.
  • He also attended Chilbo High School until he formally joined ENHYPEN, after which he switched schools to Hanlim Multi Art School, where he graduated on February 11th, 2022.
  • Sunoo had a crab, snail, and guppie as pets growing up.
  • He learned hapkido and piano when he was young.
  • Sunoo ranked 1st multiple times during I-Land by global votes.
  • Before debut, Sunoo was class president and a student council member at his school.
  • In the final episode of I-Land, Sunoo was the producer’s pick, having earned a total of 935,771 votes.

Hobbies and Skills

  • Sunoo can memorize songs quickly, and he has loved singing since elementary school.
  • Sunoo is often called “Stage Genius” for his ability to express himself on and off stage.
  • He loves to watch k-dramas and movies during his downtime.
  • Sunoo, along with Ni-ki, enjoy watching horror movies.
  • He is one of the members who post the most amount of selfies on ENHYPEN’s social media.
  • Along with Jungwon, he was the DJ at the Korea Educational Broadcasting System (KEBS) show, “Listen”! (청소년 소통 프로젝트 <경청>) from Feb 13 to June 26, 2022.
  • Sunoo is often called “sunshine” or “happy virus” by fans for his ability to lighten the mood and bring smiles to people’s faces.

Likes and Dislikes

  • Sunoo likes mint-chocolate flavored desserts.
  • Together with Heeseung, their unit is called ‘Spicyz’ due to their love of spicy food.
  • He likes mellow music, scented candles, and anything that is calming.
  • Sunoo likes flowers and nature, and he often takes scenic photos and shares them with Engenes on social media.
  • Sunoo loves performing, and he has said the stage is “the place I always wanted to be and dreamed of. Whenever I’m on stage, I feel extremely happy.”
  • Sunoo has always been interested in hairstyling since he was young. During the I-land broadcast, Sunoo was seen doing hair for multiple members. He has also done hairstyling for the BE:LIFT staff for fun.
  • He dislikes being alone. He prefers being around people and getting to know them.
  • Sunoo’s favorite book is “Son, Read This When You’re Tired of Life” by Yoon Taejin. He likes the book because it gave him solutions to his worries.
  • Sunoo’s favorite song is BOL4 - “To My Youth”, and his favorite Enhypen song is “Given-Taken.”
  • One of Sunoo’s favorite animations is “Leafie: a Hen Into The Wild” (2011). He watched it when he was in elementary school, and his favorite line from it is “Greenie, try to follow me. Mom will be watching you.”

Personality

  • Sunoo’s life motto is “Live every day happily.”
  • He describes himself as someone who is “bright and energetic.”
  • He is an animated story-teller, and he often uses sound effects and his hands to emphasize his points.
  • Fans considered his duality as one of his charm points. He is also praised widely for his aegyo.
  • Sunoo is empathetic and cares for others, and Jay once describes him as “a friend who does things for him without ever complaining.”
  • He is a positive thinker that is rooted in reality.
  • Sunoo considers himself a careful decision maker.
  • He is the type of person that never gives up when he has a goal in mind.

Random Facts

  • Sunoo made his own show on Vlive called “SUNOO’s Curiosity Research Center,” where he interviewed ENHYPEN members.
  • He was a guest for the popular Korean cooking show “The Best Cooking Secrets Studio.”
  • Sunoo started auditioning for various agencies starting in 8th grade.
  • He has an older sister, who was his initial inspiration for liking scented products.
  • His middle school schoolmates referred to him as “Sam-Oh oppa” (Year 3, Class 5 Handsome Oppa).
  • If he was an animal, Sunoo said he would be a ‘desert fox’.
  • Sunoo described himself as Vitamin, Charming, Handsome.
  • He is good at responding to members’ texts according to Sunghoon.
  • Even in elementary school, Sunoo told everyone he would become a celebrity. He said his friends weren’t surprised when he debuted.
  • Sunoo is the type that if he is given work to do, he will concentrate on getting it done quickly.
